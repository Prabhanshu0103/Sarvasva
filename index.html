🗿Educational Visit To Infantry Museum

The Department of Forensic Science at Government Holkar Science College, Indore, led by Dr. Geetha Sarasan, organized an educational visit to the Infantry Museum, Mhow

Full description: to strengthen experiential learning and connect classroom theory with real‑world military history. Students explored India’s first dedicated military museum—17 themed rooms with 3D models, murals, archival records, regiment galleries, the Kargil War room, immersive dioramas, outdoor tanks and vehicles, and a 106‑foot national flag—gaining insights into documentation, preservation, research, and the evolution of the Indian Infantry.
